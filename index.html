About Me

ABOUT ME

My name is Luciauna (Luci) Heaton, and I am 9 years old. I have been dancing for almost my whole life (6 years to be exact)! My mom took me to see the production of Thriller by Odyssey Dance Company here in my home state of Utah, when I was the age of 3. That was the day I knew I wanted to be a dancer! My mom then enrolled me into rec classes at Elite Academy, to see if I enjoyed dancing and being on stage. I didn't just enjoy it; I LOVED it, and knew I wanted learn more about dancing. My coaches encouraged me to try-out for a Competition Team the following year, and so thats what I did. I was so excited to find out that I made the team, and I have been dancing Competition ever since. Dance has given me so many wonderful things. I made the very best friends here, who became my dance sisters; they became a part of my family. Dance has given me strength and confidence. There is nothing I love more than being at my studio with my dance sisters or performing on stage!
