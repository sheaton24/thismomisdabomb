My name is Luciauna (Luci) Heaton, and I am 9 years old. I have been training at Elite Academy in all different styles of dance; Ballet, Jazz, Hip Hop and lyrical. I have been training in tumbling at Elite Academy for 3 years and at Wasatch Trampoline and Tumbling for 1 year. I have three things in my life that I am passionate about; my family, my friends and Dance! My mom took me to see the production of Thriller by Odyssey Dance Company here in my home state of Utah, when I was the age of 3. That was the day I knew I wanted to be a dancer! My mom then enrolled me into rec classes at Elite Academy, to see if I enjoyed dancing and being on stage. I didn't just enjoy it; I LOVED it, and knew I wanted to learn more about dancing. My coaches encouraged me to try-out for a Competition Team the following year, and so thats what I did. I was so excited to find out that I made the team, and I have been dancing Competition ever since. Dance has given me so many wonderful things. I made the very best friends here, who became my dance sisters; they became a part of my family. Dance has given me strength and confidence. There is nothing I love more than being at my studio with my dance sisters or performing on stage!
